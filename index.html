The specially crafted dynamic driver designed by Apple unfolds a broad spectrum of frequencies, unveiling intricate details in every sound. Experience your favorite songs with a newfound texture and accuracy that was previously unheard.

Revolutionizing your listening experience.

AirPods Max employ six external microphones to effectively eliminate unwanted ambient noise. Additionally, two internal microphones gauge the sounds you're immersed in. Thanks to beamforming technology, your voice is distinctly captured during phone calls, ensuring clarity even in windy conditions.

Clear Harmony.

Seamless, distortion-free playback is achieved through the dual-neodymium ring magnet motor, inspired by high-end floor-standing speakers. This design minimizes total harmonic distortion across the entire audible range, ensuring crystal-clear playback remains consistent, even at full volume.

Immerse yourself in a cinematic atmosphere for movies and shows with Personalized Spatial Audio featuring dynamic head tracking. This innovative technology utilizes the integrated gyroscopes and accelerometers in AirPods Max, coupled with your iPhone, iPad, Mac, or Apple TV, to follow the nuanced movements of your head, ensuring that the audio surrounds you seamlessly.

Experience a three-dimensional listening journey with Apple Music, as Personalized Spatial Audio, in conjunction with dynamic head tracking, positions sound in every direction, delivering an unparalleled auditory experience.
